Resource Request Form

Welcome to the Community Resource Commons. This form allows you to request essential items sustainably managed and distributed to meet the needs of all community members.

To ensure fair access, please note that:

  • Requests are fulfilled every day to allow for efficient distribution. Delivery times may vary depending on item availability.
  • Each item has a maximum request limit based on expected individual needs. Please request only what you need.
  • To prevent shortages, requests for items in high demand may be temporarily limited. The system restocks items regularly based on community production and imports.
  • Some items, such as fresh produce, dairy, and meat, may spoil over time. Please be considerate when ordering perishable goods in large quantities to avoid unnecessary waste.
  • We ask all members to consider others' needs, allowing us to collectively maintain a steady supply of essentials.
